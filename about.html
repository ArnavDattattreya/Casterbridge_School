Our School History

R.S. Casterbridge School was established in 2016 with the sole purpose of imparting knowledge and shaping young, energetic individuals ready to take on the world.

Founded by the Ramjanm Singh Education and Welfare Trust, the school operates under the trust’s administration to blend knowledge and experience, producing global citizens.

The school's philosophy is to provide career-oriented, student-centered education, fostering critical thinking, moral values, and skills for excelling in a global community.
