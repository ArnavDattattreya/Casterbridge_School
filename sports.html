SPORTS

The R.S. Casterbridge School Campus has
a specially made Sports area which includes
indoor & Outdoor Games.
The School also holds sports activities on a regular basis.
Weekly classes are allocated for dance, music & painting.
These classes are crafted in a manner so that students are informed about all kinds of arts and culture.

House System

The students are divided into four houses that focus on
most of the activities outside the classroom
and serve to promote the spirit of friendly and healthy
competitions in sporting, literary & cultural activities.
They also help to promote
feelings of belongingness amongst the members of the school
who are from different backgrounds.

The House System is divided into four Teams:

  • 1. Gandhi House
  • 2. Nehru House
  • 3. Patel House
  • 4. Tagore House
